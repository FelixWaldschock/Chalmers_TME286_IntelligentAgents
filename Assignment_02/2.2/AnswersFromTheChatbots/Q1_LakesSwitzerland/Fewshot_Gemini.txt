Name the 5 largest lakes that lay exclusively in switzerland. Keep In mind, that for a lake to lay exclusively in Switzerland, it must not border to another country.

Lake Neuchâtel: The largest lake entirely within Switzerland. It has a surface area of 218 square kilometers.
